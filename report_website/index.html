Welcome to Companio

Abstract

Many individuals, especially the elderly and those with cognitive impairments, struggle with memory retention and maintaining engaging interactions. Loneliness is also a growing issue, and meaningful companionship can have a tangible impact on mental health. However, existing AI companions often lack long-term memory retention, making conversations feel disconnected. Additionally, most solutions are either too resource-intensive or locked behind expensive paywalls, limiting accessibility.

Companio, developed in collaboration with Avanade and IBM, addresses these challenges by integrating Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) for long-term chat history retention. It offers both a lightweight, offline, free version and an online, paid version with advanced features. The platform is powered by Microsoft's Semantic Kernel, enabling customizable avatars, site configurations, and real-time text-to-speech and speech-to-text interactions. Additionally, memory-enhancing games are incorporated to promote cognitive well-being.

By combining personalized AI companionship with cognitive support tools, Companio creates a more engaging and effective virtual assistant experience. Its accessibility across offline and online environments ensures that users from all backgrounds can benefit from an intelligent, adaptable, and cost-effective companion, ultimately improving daily interactions and memory retention. It also has the potential to revolutionize the use of AI in the healthcare industry, introducing new ways it can be leveraged to support cognitive health and well-being.
